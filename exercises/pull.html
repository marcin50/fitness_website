Pull Exercises

Please use the table below as a guideline for the amount of repetitions required to achieve a certain goal:

Goal Speed Repititions Weights Rest Between sets
Strength Slow 1-6 Max/Heavy 3-5 min
Hypertrophy Medium 7-12 Medium/Light 1-3 min
Endurace Fast 12+ Light/None 30-60 sec

Deadlift

The deadlift is the single most important pull exercise. It uses the entire back glutes and legs with specific focus on the lower back. Deadlifts require a lot of practice to perform safely and you should start on lower weights to improve form before attempting a max weight lift.

Pull-ups

Pulls-ups are my second most favorite pull exercise because they target the muscles that deadlifts miss and serve as a great compliment. They mainly target the latissimus dorsi muscles as well as a few secondary muscles.
