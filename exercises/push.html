Push Exercises

Please use the table below as a guideline for the amount of repetitions required to achieve a certain goal:

Goal Speed Repititions Weights Rest Between sets
Strength Slow 1-6 Max/Heavy 3-5 min
Hypertrophy Medium 7-12 Medium/Light 1-3 min
Endurace Fast 12+ Light/None 30-60 sec

Bench Press

The bench press is my favorite push exercise because it provides lots of upper body strength and it targets many secondary muscles.

Incline Bench Press

The incline bench press compliment the regular bench press because it targets a different region of similar muscle to the chest press. Particularly, the upper chest and shoulders.
