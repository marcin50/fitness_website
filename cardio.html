Cardio

Cardio is just as important as any bodybuilding exercise because it provides a wide range of benefits to overall health. Running in particular is beneficial to every part of the human body and has shown to improve:

  • circulation
  • brain health
  • flexibility
  • weight
  • anxiety problems
  • blood pressure
  • sleep
